ABOUT

Welcome to Live Recreational. Serving GTA and surrounding area for canoe, stand up paddleboards, kayaks, bikes, camping and gear transport rentals from Brampton, Ontario

Book online by using the Contact Page or email directly at liverecreational@gmail.com

Welcome all new and experienced paddlers.

Enjoy the great outdoors on the water. Offering the following equipment rentals:

  • 2 x Adult Inflatable SUP boards
  • 1 x Youth Inflatable SUP Board
  • 2 x Kayaks (single)
  • 2 x Youth Kayaks (single 140lb max)
  • 1 x Canoe (2 adults & 2 children)
  • 2 x Folding Bicycles
  • Paddles
  • Life jackets
  • Electric SUP Pump
  • Kayak Carrier
  • Roof Box
  • Roof Bag Carriers
  • Hitch Mounted Carriers
  • Utility Trailer
  • PWC Trailer
  • PWC Cart

Feel free to reach out if you have any additional questions or inquiries
